I study the economics of climate change, with a focus on quantifying the social costs of greenhouse gas emissions and incorporating biodiversity and ecosystem feedbacks into integrated assessment models. My work bridges environmental science and policy, drawing on statistical modeling, geospatial analysis, and reproducible research practices.
